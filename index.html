Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with thise festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeats
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cupe extra virgin oil olive, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrapes rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought

In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix tougheter all the dought ingredients until the flours is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dought, thought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
